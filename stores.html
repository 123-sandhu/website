Stores Module

Maintain optimal inventory levels with real-time stock visibility for faster, informed decisions.

Key Features

  • Stores & Inventory can be maintained at optimal levels with real-time stock availability for all users.
  • Access stock instantly without dependence on Stores or Accounts for better transparency and faster decisions.
  • Get stock with a click and have more time for stock checks.
  • A, B, C analysis of stock.
  • Categorize stock into groups for easy analysis as per industry norms.
  • Maintain Minimum (ROL) stock for critical items.
  • Track stock at Stores, Shop Floor, JW Vendors, Service Engineers, etc.
  • Assign rack location for easy storing & retrieving.
  • Maintain source document for all inward/outward operations with real-time inventory reports.
