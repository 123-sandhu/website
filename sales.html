Sales Module

Empower your sales team to manage leads, close deals, track performance, and forecast sales effectively.

Key Features

  • Maintain up-to-date information on prospects, close more deals, follow up with operations for timely delivery, and keep customers informed.
  • Systematic management of all leads and enquiries (no lost or forgotten enquiries).
  • Stages of Quotation tracking (Sales funnel management).
  • Maintain customer discussion details for each enquiry for timely action.
  • Convert Quotation to Order with one click.
  • Track sales team performance (Leads generated/Conversion).
  • Outstanding Receivables ageing analysis for faster collections.
  • Quotations Won/Lost analysis.
  • Sales forecasting (next month/quarter sales probability).
